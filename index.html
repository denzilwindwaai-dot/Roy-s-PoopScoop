Terms of Service

Welcome to Roy's PoopScoop. By subscribing to or booking our yard hygiene services, you agree to the following operational parameters:

1. Property Access & Security

Yards, service gates, and properties must be fully accessible on your scheduled service day. If our team arrives and cannot access the area due to locked gates or restricted estate codes, the visit will be forfeited but still billed at your full standard rate.

2. Pet Safety & Aggression

For the safety of our operators, aggressive or highly protective dogs must be safely secured inside the house or a separate enclosure during our service window. We reserve the right to decline entry if an unsecured dog poses a perceived threat.

3. Lawn Maintenance Requirements

To ensure we locate and remove all pet waste effectively, lawns must be kept cut to a reasonable, maintained length. Excessively long or un-mowed grass drastically reduces visibility; while we perform thorough sweeps, we cannot be held liable for missed waste hidden in overgrown brush or long grass profiles.

4. Billing & Payments

All recurring residential monthly retainer plans are billed and strictly payable in advance via Electronic Funds Transfer (EFT). Ad-hoc holiday rental visits or initial cleanup surcharges are payable immediately upon invoice receipt.

5. Weather and Service Delays

Severe West Coast weather events (high wind storms or torrential rain) may occasionally interrupt scheduled service routes. In these instances, services will be shifted to the next immediate safe clearing day to ensure thorough yard sanitization.

6. Service Exclusions

Roy's PoopScoop is strictly a specialized property hygiene and animal waste clearing business. Under no circumstances do we offer or perform lawn mowing, weed pulling, hedge trimming, landscaping, or standard green garden waste removal.
